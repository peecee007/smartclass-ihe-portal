About the Program

Bridging the Digital Gap in Rural Enugu

Many schools in Ihe and Awgu LGA lack access to practical digital training. This NYSC-backed project directly addresses that gap by delivering hands-on computer literacy skills to students and teachers alike.

  • ✓
    Practical, hands-on learning sessions — not just theory
  • ✓
    Microsoft Word & Excel training for real-world use
  • ✓
    Internet literacy and safe browsing fundamentals
  • ✓
    Official certificates issued to all participants
  • ✓
    Aligned with NYSC community development goals

What We'll Cover

Training Curriculum

Five focused modules delivered over three weeks with hands-on practical sessions after every lesson.

01
Introduction to Computers
Hardware, software, operating systems, mouse & keyboard basics
02
Microsoft Word
Typing, formatting, document creation, printing, and templates
03
Microsoft Excel
Data entry, formulas, calculations, and basic spreadsheet management
04
Internet Basics
Browsing, email, research skills, and safe internet use
05
Hands-On Practical Sessions
Real-world tasks, mini-projects, and certification assessments
